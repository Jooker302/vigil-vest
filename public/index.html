About us

SafeKey (SKY) is a cutting-edge cryptocurrency token that prioritizes security in the digital asset landscape. With a total supply of 1 billion tokens, SafeKey offers robust security features, including encryption and multi-factor authentication, aiming to protect investors and users from cyber threats. Its governance functionality empowers token holders to actively shape the project's future, while token vesting, staking rewards, and a burn mechanism promote long-term commitment and economic sustainability. Fair public sales and a reserve fund underscore SafeKey's commitment to community fairness and resilience in the face of unforeseen challenges, making it a promising project dedicated to safeguarding digital assets and enhancing trust within the cryptocurrency market.

Tokenomics

Initial Distribution

- Team and Development: 20% (200,000,000 SAFEKEY)
- Advisors and Partnerships: 10% (100,000,000 SAFEKEY)
- Reserve Fund: 15% (150,000,000 SAFEKEY)
- Marketing and Community Building: 10% (100,000,000 SAFEKEY)
- Public Sale: 20% (200,000,000 SAFEKEY)
- Liquidity Pool: 10% (100,000,000 SAFEKEY)
- Staking Rewards: 15% (150,000,000 SAFEKEY)

Utility and Governance

- SafeKey can be used for transaction fees within the SafeKey ecosystem.
- It serves as a governance token, allowing holders to participate in decision-making processes.

Staking Rewards

- Token holders can stake their SAFEKEY tokens to earn rewards.
- The staking rewards will be distributed from the Staking Rewards allocation.

Burning Mechanism & Vesting

- A portion of transaction fees is burned, reducing the total supply over time.
- Team and Development tokens have a vesting period of 4 years.
- Advisors and Partnerships tokens have a vesting period of 2 years.

Community Rewards & Reserve Fund

- Initiatives to reward active community members and contributors can be established from the Marketing and Community Building allocation.
- The Reserve Fund is held to support ongoing development, partnerships, and unforeseen circumstances.

Liquidity Provision on & Public Sale

- A portion of the Liquidity Pool tokens may be provided to decentralized exchanges to ensure liquidity.
- The public sale may be conducted through multiple rounds or auctions to ensure fair distribution.
